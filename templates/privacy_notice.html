Privacy Notice

1. Information Collection

The Educational Ransomware Simulation tool collects the following information:

  • File metadata from files you choose to analyze or process
  • Encryption/decryption activity logs
  • Technical information about your environment for debugging purposes

2. Use of Information

The information collected is used for:

  • Improving the simulation tool
  • Advancing research in ransomware detection and prevention
  • Generating anonymous statistics about usage patterns

3. Information Sharing

With your consent, information about the files you analyze may be shared with the cybersecurity community. This helps advance collective knowledge about ransomware behavior and defense mechanisms. We never share personal information or file contents.

4. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect the information collected. However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

5. User Responsibilities

You are responsible for ensuring that you do not submit files containing sensitive, confidential, or personal information. We are not responsible for the security of any such information if you choose to submit it.

6. Data Retention

We retain log data and file metadata for a limited period necessary for research and improvement purposes.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to request access to the information we have collected about your usage of the tool, and to request that we delete such information.

8. Changes to This Privacy Notice

We may update this privacy notice from time to time. We will notify users of any material changes by posting the new privacy notice on this page.
